# Help & Support

Welcome! This page answers common questions for our apps — **Magic Lab**, **Dex Lab**, **Magic Memories**, **Fine Art Lab**, and **Battle Toys**. These tips apply to all of them.

## How it works

1. **Take or choose a photo** of your subject — a toy, pet, trading card, old photograph, or piece of artwork, depending on the app.
2. **Pick an animation style** from the menu.
3. **Wait a few seconds** while our AI creates your video, then **save or share** it.

## Credits

- Animations are created using **credits**.
- **Your first generation is free**, so you can try the app before buying anything.
- You can buy more credits at any time from the in-app store.
- **Credits are shared across the whole family of apps.** One account gives you a single credit balance you can use in Magic Lab, Dex Lab, Magic Memories, Fine Art Lab, and Battle Toys.

## Purchases

- **Restoring your credits:** Credits are tied to your account, so just sign in with the same Google or Apple account on any device to see your balance.
- **A generation failed — was I charged?** If an animation fails, the credits are automatically returned to your balance. If you think a credit was lost, contact us.
- **Refunds** for purchases are handled by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store) under their own policies.

## Saving & sharing

When your video is ready, you can save it to your device's photo gallery and share it to any app.

## Account & sign-in

- Sign in with **Google** or **Apple**. We never see your password.
- Your credit balance and history are linked to this account.

## Troubleshooting

- **My video didn't generate, or it's taking a long time.** Check your internet connection and try again. Busy periods can occasionally slow things down. Failed generations never cost credits.
- **The result doesn't look right.** Use a clear, well-lit photo where the subject fills most of the frame, without heavy glare or obstructions.
- **I can't see my credits.** Make sure you're signed in with the same account you used to buy them.
- **The app says it's under maintenance.** We occasionally pause the service for upgrades. Please try again a little later.

## Privacy

Your photos are used only to create your video and are not stored permanently. Read our full [Privacy Policy](https://github.com/Fnordcorps/MAGIC-LAB-Privacy-Policy/blob/main/PRIVACY_POLICY.md).
